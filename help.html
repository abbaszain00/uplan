Capabilities

What UPlan can do

UPlan is more than a search tool — it's a conversational planner that gets to know you over time.

🧠

Remembers you

Your budget, location, and interests are saved. Come back tomorrow and UPlan already knows you.

🎟️

Live event search

Pulls real upcoming events from Ticketmaster based on your location, date, and interests.

✅

Vets recommendations

Before showing results, UPlan checks them against your preferences — no irrelevant suggestions.

💬

Thinks with you

Not sure what hobby to try? UPlan can brainstorm ideas, ask the right questions, and help you explore.

Examples

How to talk to UPlan

UPlan works best when you're honest about what you want. Here are two example sessions showing how it learns and adapts.

Session 1 — Discovering music events
You
I'm looking to start a new hobby. I've always been interested in live music, but I'm a student on a very tight budget — free or ultra-low-cost only.
🎯
That sounds brilliant! I've noted that keeping things budget-friendly is the top priority. Are you based anywhere specific? I can look up what's on near you.
You
I'm in London for the next two weeks. Any live gig will do.
🎯
Here are some events coming up in London that fit your vibe — I've filtered for the most accessible options: Live Jazz at Piano Bar Soho (29 Apr), Round 41 Jazz & Fusion Night (29 Apr), Producers Playground — Nu-Soul & UK Beats (30 Apr). Prices aren't listed so I'd recommend checking the links, but these venues often have free or pay-what-you-want entry. Want me to filter further?
Session 2 — New interest, same memory
🎯
Welcome back! I remember you're looking for free events in London. Still on the music scene, or has something changed?
You
Actually I want to try something more active — like a sport. I'm free this Friday.
🎯
Love it — sport is great for zero budget too! Let me look up what's happening in London this Friday... Here are some options: ITTF World Table Tennis Championships at Copper Box Arena, Arsenal Women vs Leicester City at Emirates. Want me to narrow it down by type of sport?
New session — UPlan remembered your location & budget automatically

Tips

Getting the most out of UPlan

01

Be upfront about budget

Mention your budget early — UPlan will filter everything to match it from that point on.

02

Say your location

UPlan needs a city to search events. Once told, it'll remember for future sessions.

03

Change your mind freely

Tell UPlan you've moved on from an interest and it'll update what it knows about you.

04

Ask what it knows

Type "What do you know about me?" at any time to see your stored preferences.

05

Give it dates

"I'm free this Friday" or "I'm in London for two weeks" helps UPlan find timely events.

06

Just chat

No need for commands. Talk naturally — UPlan will figure out what you need.
